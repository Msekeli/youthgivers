About Us

CHEERFUL GIVERS COMMUNITY OUTREACH

Cheerful Givers Community Outreach is a group of young people with the same vision of giving back to the community. This organization was established in mid-June 2018, under the leadership of senior pastors M&T. Ndzingo at God's Harvest Center Ministries. God's Harvest Centre Ministries is the church which is fully focused on soul winning and its mission is to preach the word the Word of God all over the world and make the disciples of Jesus Christ. Through going around preaching the Good News of Jesus Christ, having community outreach. The church discovered a need in the community, whereby people are facing issues of inequality, poverty, unemployment, crime, drugs, violence and etc. Cheerful Givers was established to fight against these issues by giving back to the community, expressing the love of God to his people and to make great impact to the community.



OUR AIM

To flatten the curve on the challenges that young people are facing in our community, such as inequality, poverty, unemployment, crime (drugs, violence) etc.

MISSION

  • Attend the crucial & burning issues that affect young people.
  • To motivate & encourage young people in all areas of their lives.
  • To organize events & outreaches which empower young people.
  • Assist young people to become who they were destined to be.
  • Learning & Monitoring youth to be guided by the word of God.
  • To emphasize the importance & value of the church in the community.

VALUES

  • Humanity
  • Unity
  • Transparency
  • Respect
  • Integrity & love

What We Do...

CHEERFUL GIVERS COMMUNITY OUTREACH


We Offer
  • After School Classes
  • Career Exploration
  • Prayer Meetings
  • Soup Kitchen
  • Small Business Seminars

Afternoon school program: Dedicated in promoting academic excellence through assisting children and adults in their journey of finding their career for God's glory.

Business Seminars program: Assisting people who have their small businesses and those who have a vision of starting their own business. Giving them relevant information that will make them become successful businessman/businesswoman.

Prayer meetings program: Offer spiritual, emotional and physical counseling

Talents to be embraced & Christ Centered Movies: Discover hidden talents and embraced them. Assisting people to become who they were destined to be and to guided by the word of God.

Soup kitchen program: Fighting against the spirit of poverty by assisting with food parcel, clothes & etc
